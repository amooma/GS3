Germany
========================

The area codes for Germany (+49) are
those provided officially by the german "Bundesnetzagentur"
("BNetzA").
http://www.bundesnetzagentur.de/enid/79f2a1a0a7b89ff7d3a26f030ce2baeb,0/Verzeichnisse/ONB_1gh.html
ONB-Verzeichnis 2007
(is_ac=1)

We have merged in other non-area prefixes as described in
http://www.bundesnetzagentur.de/enid/541c0fcac899f1451f2783bb54f0dad7,0/Nummernverwaltung/Uebersicht_Nummernraum_1hz.html
(is_ac=0)


USA
========================

Area codes for the USA are available from the "North American
Numbering Plan Administration" ("NANPA") at
http://www.nanpa.com/area_codes/index.html

US area codes are easily recognizable because they always have 3
digits. The format is NXX, where N is [2-9] and X is [0-9].
